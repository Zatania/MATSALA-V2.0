{# Privacy Policy Modal #}
Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2025

1. Introduction

The MATSALA Assistance System (“System”) is developed to provide financial aid to students in need at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP). We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) and university policies on data protection.

2. Information We Collect
  • Full Name
  • Student ID Number
  • Email Address
  • GCash Number
  • Username and Password
  • Facial Scan/Image
  • Uploaded supporting documents for assistance requests
  • Optional: Donor name and GCash reference number
3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Create secure accounts
  • Authenticate users via facial recognition
  • Validate assistance requests
  • Prevent fraud and duplicate accounts
  • Maintain transaction logs
  • Report to authorized PUP officials
4. Facial Recognition

Facial scans are used to ensure that only one verified account is created per student. Multiple accounts using the same face will be flagged, and duplicate claims will be rejected.

5. Data Sharing

Your data is not sold or traded. It is only accessible to authorized system administrators and relevant university officials when necessary.

6. Data Retention

Information is retained in accordance with PUP policy and legal requirements.

7. Security Measures

We employ technical and administrative safeguards to protect your data. Manual fund disbursement is used to avoid API-related risks.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data and to file a complaint with the NPC for violations. Contact the PUP Data Protection Office to exercise your rights.

{# Terms and Conditions Modal #}
Terms and Conditions

Effective Date: June 2025

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using the MATSALA System, you agree to these Terms and Conditions and all applicable university policies.

2. Eligibility

Only current PUP students may register. The system enforces a one-account-per-student rule using facial recognition.

3. Beneficiary Responsibilities
  • Provide accurate details
  • Submit valid proof for assistance
  • Use your own or landlord's verified GCash number
  • Adhere to the 2-week assistance claim interval
4. Donor Use
  • Donations can be anonymous or registered
  • GCash donations require reference number submission
  • Donations are non-refundable
5. Admin and System Control
  • Admins may approve or reject claims
  • All transactions are logged
  • GCash disbursement is manual for added security
6. System Misuse

Creating multiple accounts, submitting fake documents, or impersonation will lead to disqualification and may be escalated to PUP authorities.

7. Amendments

We reserve the right to update this document. Changes will be published on the system portal or sent via email.
