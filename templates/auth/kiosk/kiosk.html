How to Use

For donation, you can either:

  • Donate through coins (press Coin Slot)
  • Or GCash QR Code (press GCash QR)

For assistance, you can either:

  • Scan your face (press Facial Scan)
  • Or log in manually (press Account Login)

If you have trouble with face recognition, choose “Login” instead.
